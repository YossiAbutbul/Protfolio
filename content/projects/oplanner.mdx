## The problem

Juggling a full CS degree alongside work means Notion, Google Calendar, and a stack of sticky notes eventually lose the plot. I wanted a planner that spoke the language of a semester: courses, assignments, due dates, milestones, and *how far through the term you actually are*.

## What I built

A full-stack web app: React + TypeScript on the front end, Firebase on the back, responsive UI with progress-visualization dashboards. Built around semester-scoped planning, not generic to-do lists.

## Highlights

- **Reusable React components** — progress rings, course cards, heatmap of upcoming load.
- **Firebase-backed state** — realtime sync across devices without a custom server.
- **Responsive UI** — the same planner reads cleanly on phone during a commute and on laptop during a study session.
